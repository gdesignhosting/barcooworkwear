About Us

About Barcoo Workwear

Barcoo Workwear is a wholly owned Australian business that specialises in designing and manufacturing hi vis safety shirts and pants for the mining and construction industries.

We use next generation fabric that is cool, light and strong that will withstand the rigours associated with the mining and construction fields.

All our range of clothing is made in Sri Lanka where they have strict rules regarding the use of child labour and working conditions. Their workmanship is superior to many other garment manufacturing countries so you can be assured of Barcoo's quality.

At Barcoo we are serious about men’s health issues and are committed to making men aware of the importance of annual check up with their GP. We have teamed up with The Princess Alexandria Hospital Research Foundation (PA Research Foundation) to promote men’s health. On the inside of each shirt is printed a reminder to have annual check up. Click here to view printed message.

As well as this, for every shirt sold, Barcoo Workwear contributes to the PA Research Foundation’s research into prostate cancer.
