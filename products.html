Shirts

All shirts in the Barcool range are made 100% cotton woven ripstop fabric 145 gsm, with cooling vents under the arms and upper back, 2 buttoned pockets on chest with a pen hole on left pocket.

All styles come with or without reflective tape and comply with AS/NZS 4602.1:2011 and AS/NZS 1906.4:2010 along with a UPF factor of 50+

Cooler

Not only does the 'Barcool' range of shirts have cooling air vents sewn into the armpits (image) and upper back, our shirts are made from a 100% cotton fabric that has breathing capabilities. The collar has an extension that gives extra protection for the neck from the sun. This all leads for more comfort to the wearer.

Stronger

The 'Barcool' range of shirts are made from a 100% cotton woven fabric, using a special reinforcing technique that makes them resistant to tearing and ripping. Advantages of ripstop are the favourable strength to weight ratio which allows for a strong lightweight shirt and that small tears cannot easily spread.

Value

Barcoo Workwear has introduced the best value for money safety shirt that doesn’t compromise on quality. In fact we believe our workmanship is superior along with a design which is cooler and stronger for Australia’s harsh conditions. The best quality, light weight fabric has been used to produce these shirts.
